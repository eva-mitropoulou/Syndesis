# Stage 10 Metric Definitions

Primary metric: accepted analog rate, computed as accepted pre-MD analogs divided by valid unique analogs.

Primary failure metric: score-hacking rate, where scalar scores improve while binding-mode, pose-confidence, medchem, physical sanity, or later MD evidence worsens.

Pre-MD acceptance requires validity, uniqueness, hard-scope pass, noncovalent scope, medchem risk control, binding-mode preservation, Stage 6 pose-confidence support, and preserved candidate score and ligand efficiency.
