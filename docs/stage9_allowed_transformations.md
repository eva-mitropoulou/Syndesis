# Stage 9 Allowed Transformations

Enabled conservative classes:

- R-group replacement
- small substituent scan
- heteroatom swap
- ring decoration
- phenyl-to-heteroaryl replacement
- halogen scan
- methyl, ethyl, F, Cl, OMe, CN, and CF3 scans where chemically valid
- solubilizing tail tuning
- rotatable-bond reduction
- hydrogen-bond donor and acceptor tuning
- matched molecular pair transformations
- BRICS recombination
- conservative bioisostere replacements
- linker replacement only for linker-like regions

Disallowed in v1:

- covalent EGFR warhead introduction
- acrylamide, propiolamide, and Michael-acceptor Cys797 warheads
- allosteric design
- peptide or macrocycle expansion unless configured
- large core demolition outside a labeled scaffold-hop experiment
- metal-containing compounds
- invalid-valence or severely reactive molecules
