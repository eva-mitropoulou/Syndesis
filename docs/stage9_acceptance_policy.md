# Stage 9 Acceptance Policy

Accepted analogs must satisfy all hard checks:

- valid RDKit molecule
- v1 reversible noncovalent EGFR scope
- no covalent warhead
- no severe reactive flag
- hard property window pass
- dockable in the Stage 8 mini-screen
- GNINA-supported
- ProLIF binding-mode-consistent
- Stage 6 pose-confidence-positive
- candidate score and ligand efficiency preserved or improved
- no score-hacking flag

Docking-score improvement alone is insufficient. Binding-mode preservation and pose-confidence support are required for acceptance.
