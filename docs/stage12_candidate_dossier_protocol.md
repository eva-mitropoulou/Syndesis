# Stage 12 Candidate Dossier Protocol

Stage 12 builds audit-ready candidate evidence packages from completed upstream tables. It does not run new docking, GNINA rescoring, ProLIF scoring, MD, model training, or agent analog generation.

Each dossier is generated from the final candidate table, candidate card JSON, Stage 7 provenance, Stage 8 screening evidence, Stage 9 analog outcomes, Stage 10 benchmark outputs, and Stage 11 MD/parameterization outputs when available.

Known controls are reported as diagnostic controls. Rejected analogs may be included as negative-control examples. Prospective candidate claims require source-aware separation and explicit limitations.
